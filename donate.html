Meet Ruby

Help more shelter pets like Ruby get the second chance they deserve.

Ruby was one of hundreds of dogs sitting in an overcrowded shelter, healthy and adoptable, but running out of time simply due to a lack of space and low adoption rates.

Just by changing her location, Ruby went from overlooked and at-risk to a forever home, with all the toys and love she could ever need.

That is what your donation makes possible. Every transport trip, every surgery, every mile driven by one of our volunteers exists because people like you believe that we can create a positive shift in animal welfare.
