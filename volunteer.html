Open Roles

Where do you fit?

Every role below is open year-round. Some need more vetting than others — we'll walk you through everything.

Drive & Transport

Transport Driver

Our most critical need. Drivers take multi-day road trips moving animals from overcrowded shelters to our Pacific Northwest partners. Trips typically run 1–2 days. You will be paired with another volunteer and provided with everything you need — fuel, lodging, and transport supplies are covered.

Must be 21+ Clean driving record Comfortable with animals 1–2 day availability

Transport Pre & Post

Transport Prep & Clean

Everything that happens before and after the road trip. Crate building and van packing before departure, then crate cleaning and sanitization after animals arrive safely. Based locally in Bend. A great fit if you prefer hands-on tasks and want to support transports without the travel.

Local to Bend Physical tasks involved Flexible scheduling

Common Questions

What people ask us

Do I need experience with animals?

For most roles, no. A genuine love for animals and the ability to stay calm around them is enough. Drivers are paired with experienced volunteers on their first trip.

Are expenses covered for transport drivers?

Yes — fuel, lodging, and transport costs are covered by Furry Freight. You should not be out of pocket for a trip.

How long are transport trips?

Most trips run 1–2 days depending on the pickup location. We try to schedule in advance so you can plan around your life.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes — social media, grant writing, and content creation can all be done from anywhere. Several of our most consistent contributors are fully remote.

I can only help occasionally. Is that okay?

Absolutely. We welcome one-time event helpers just as much as regular monthly drivers. Any contribution moves us forward.

What if I'm not sure which role fits me?

Fill out the sign-up form and tell us a bit about yourself. We'll figure it out together — there are no wrong answers.
