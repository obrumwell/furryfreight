Support the Animals

Help us keep rolling.

Every dollar, every bottle returned, every shared post helps save a life. Here is every way you can support Furry Freight.

Make a Donation

Your gift goes straight to the animals.

We are 100% volunteer run. Every dollar you donate funds transport trips and spay/neuter surgeries for pets who need them most.

One-time gifts help us respond quickly. Monthly giving helps us plan ahead and commit to more transports and more clinic days.

Want to sponsor a spay or neuter surgery? For $70, you can prevent hundreds of animals from entering an already maxed shelter system. Add a note in the comments that says "Spay it Forward" and we will direct your gift there.

More Ways to Help

Other ways to support Furry Freight

Not everyone can give finanically, we absoletly get that. Here are other ways you can lift up our organization.

Oregon BottleDrop

Pick up blue bags from us, fill them with returnables, and drop them off at any BottleDrop center. The credit comes straight to Furry Freight at no cost to you.

Learn How

Fred Meyer Rewards

Link your Fred Meyer Shopper's Card to Furry Freight through the Community Rewards program. Fred Meyer donates a portion of your everyday purchases. Free to set up.

Link Your Card

Amazon Wishlist

Ship supplies directly to our team. Crates, leashes, cleaning supplies, and more. Items go straight to the volunteers keeping our animals safe on the road.

View Wishlist

GearFix Bend Consignment

Drop off your used outdoor gear at GearFix in Bend and consign it in our name. When it sells, the proceeds come to Furry Freight.

Learn About GearFix

Shop at Our Online Thrift Store

We sell donated items online. Every purchase supports our programs directly.

Shop Now

Attend Our Events

Our annual Yard Sale, Holiday Treat Sale, Egg My House, and Bingo nights are all fundraisers. Come out, have fun, and support the animals.

Get Event Updates
Every Action Makes A Difference

Your time and your voice matter too.

Some of the most valuable support we receive in the animal wellfare space costs nothing at all.

Follow and Share

Following us on Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok and sharing our posts puts our mission in front of new people. Every share is a potential adopter, volunteer, or donor.

Share Adoptable Pets

When you see a dog or cat from a local shelter or rescue posted for adoption, share it. You might be the reason that furry friend finds its home.

Volunteer Your Time

Drive, clean crates, run events, write grants, or post content. Volunteering with us, or with any local shelter or rescue, is one of the most direct ways to help animals in your community.
