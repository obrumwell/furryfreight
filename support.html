No-Cost & Low-Cost Ways to Give

Other ways to support Furry Freight

Not everyone can give financially, we get that. Here are other ways to lift up our organization.

Oregon BottleDrop

Pick up blue bags from us, fill them with returnables, and drop them off at any BottleDrop center. The credit comes straight to Furry Freight at no cost to you.

Learn How

Fred Meyer Rewards

Link your Fred Meyer Shopper's Card to Furry Freight through the Community Rewards program. Fred Meyer donates a portion of your everyday purchases. Free to set up.

Link Your Card

Amazon Wishlist

Ship supplies directly to our team. Crates, leashes, cleaning supplies, and more — straight to the volunteers keeping animals safe on the road.

View Wishlist

GearFix Bend Consignment

Drop off your used outdoor gear at GearFix in Bend and consign it in our name. When it sells, the proceeds come to Furry Freight.

Learn About GearFix

Shop Our Online Thrift Store

We sell donated items on Poshmark. Every purchase supports our programs directly.

Shop Now

Attend Our Events

Our annual Yard Sale, Holiday Treat Sale, Egg My House, and Bingo nights are all fundraisers. Come out, have fun, and support our furry friends.

Get Event Updates
Your Time & Voice

Every action makes a difference.

Some of the most valuable support we receive in the animal welfare space costs nothing at all.

Follow and Share

Following us on Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok and sharing our posts puts our mission in front of new people. Every share is a potential adopter, volunteer, or donor.

Share Adoptable Pets

When you see a dog or cat from a local shelter or rescue posted for adoption, share it. You might be the reason that animal finds its home.

Volunteer Your Time

Drive, clean crates, run events, write grants, or post content. Volunteering with us — or any local shelter or rescue — is one of the most direct ways to help animals in your community.

Every dollar goes directly to transport costs and spay/neuter surgeries. We are 100% volunteer run.
